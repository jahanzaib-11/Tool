Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image compressor

Is there a limit to how many images I can compress? +

Our free plan allows 10 compressions per day. Pro and Business plans offer unlimited compressions with larger file size limits.

Do you store my images after compression? +

No. All processing happens in your browser - we never upload your images to our servers. Your files stay completely private.

What image formats do you support? +

We support JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats. You can convert between these formats during compression.

How much can I reduce my image file sizes? +

Typical savings range from 40-90% depending on the original image quality and format. WebP usually offers the best compression.

Can I compress multiple images at once? +

Yes! Our Pro and Business plans support batch processing of multiple images simultaneously (up to 50 files on Pro, 500+ on Business).
